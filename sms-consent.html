SMS Consent & Messaging Terms

Last updated: June 22, 2026

When you provide your phone number and check the consent box on this site, you agree to receive text messages from Kick'N It w/ Keshia. Here’s exactly what you’re signing up for.

What you’ll receive

Recurring marketing and program text messages — including reset tips, updates, enrollment reminders, and information about The Grown Woman Reset.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive several messages per month depending on activity and enrollment timing.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, based on your mobile carrier and plan. We don’t charge you for the texts themselves.

How to opt out or get help

  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You’ll get one confirmation message and then no further texts.
  • Reply HELP for help, or email kiwkcustomer@gmail.com.

Consent is not a condition of purchase

You don’t have to agree to receive texts in order to enroll in the program or buy anything from us. Texting consent is entirely optional.

Carriers

Supported carriers may include major U.S. carriers and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your phone number is handled according to our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your information, and we do not share mobile opt-in data with third parties for their own marketing.

Contact

Questions about these messaging terms? Email kiwkcustomer@gmail.com.
