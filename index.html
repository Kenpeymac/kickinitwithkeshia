Questions

Frequently asked

How long is the program? +

The Grown Woman Reset runs 5 weeks. Weeks 1–4 are your live coaching sessions; Week 5 is execution week — where you take everything you've learned and put it into action. We only open it once a quarter.

What if I can't make a session live? +

Every session is recorded. You'll get the replays, so you never miss a thing.

What do I get when I join? +

After you pay, you'll receive a link to your member portal — that's where you'll access your resources and the links to join the live sessions.

What's the difference between the tiers? +

Your tier determines what you have access to — from self-led with replays, all the way up to weekly live coaching and private 1-on-1 time with Keshia.

Is this for me? +

If you're a woman 40+ who's tired of putting yourself last and ready to choose you — yes. This is your season.

Do you offer refunds? +

All sales are final. Because seats are limited and access is granted right away, we don't offer refunds. Come ready to show up for yourself.
